Have an item you just can't live without? Use 🔒 to tag it as recurring and ensure it stays in your list, week after week.
Use 📝 to add some notes and reminders about your item.
Use 🔒ALL or 🔓ALL to lock or unlock all of the list items at once.
Use 🔍 to search through your currently added items.
Use ↑↓ to sort your list items by price, from lowest to highest or, alternatively, highest to lowest.
Use 🗑️ to remove an item from your list or 🗑️ ALL to remove all of them.
Did you accidentally delete an item? Use ♻️ to recover it!
